# Job Environments

Each job has its own themed dungeon/environment. Create art for:
- Background tiles
- Obstacles/walls
- Job-specific enemies
- Atmosphere elements

## fastfood/ - "The Grinder"
- Kitchen/counter environment
- Grease, fryers, registers
- Enemies: Karen, Angry Customer, Health Inspector, Grease Fire

## warehouse/ - "The Lifter"
- Shelves, boxes, forklifts
- Industrial aesthetic
- Enemies: Forklift, Heavy Box, Angry Supervisor, Back Pain

## rideshare/ - "The Drifter"
- Car interior, streets
- Night driving vibes
- Enemies: Drunk Passenger, Traffic, Bad Rating, Car Trouble

## construction/ - "The Builder"
- Building site, scaffolding
- Hard hats, danger signs
- Enemies: Falling Debris, Power Tool, Angry Foreman, OSHA

## security/ - "The Watcher"
- Guard booth, monitors
- Night shift loneliness
- Enemies: Trespasser, Boredom, Suspicious Package, False Alarm

## callcenter/ - "The Voice"
- Cubicles, headsets
- Fluorescent lighting hell
- Enemies: Angry Caller, Dropped Call, Supervisor, Metrics

## delivery/ - "The Runner"
- Streets, apartments, packages
- Weather elements
- Enemies: Wrong Address, Dog, Apartment Stairs, No Tip

## office/ - "The Temp"
- Cubicle farm
- Printers, paperwork
- Enemies: Printer Jam, Deadline, Office Politics, Email Chain

## hospital/ - "The Orderly"
- Hospital corridors
- Clinical environment
- Enemies: Biohazard, Difficult Patient, Double Shift, Paperwork
